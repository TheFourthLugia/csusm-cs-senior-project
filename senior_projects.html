Senior Course Projects

There are two types of projects offered mainly to seniors: Course projects and Capstone projects.

Course projects

Course projects come from courses that are not part of our Capstone program. These courses can include electives such as Game Programming (CS 485) and Software Engineering (CS 370).

Capstone projects

Capstone projects come from Capstone courses that are usually the last upper-division courses required for seniors to graduate. They are typically sponsored by a third-party company or individual and focus on teams developing real-life solutions for the client's needs.
